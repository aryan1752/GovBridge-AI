Criminal cases are intense and life-changing. Whether you're facing allegations or you're a victim seeking justice, the right legal support can change everything. NyayBharat stands by you — from the first call to the final verdict.

1. What Criminal Law Covers

Criminal law deals with offences that harm individuals or society — assault, theft, harassment, domestic violence, fraud, cybercrime, and more. These matters move fast and require immediate legal attention to protect your rights.

Cases may involve physical harm, stolen property, forged documents, online threats, blackmail, NDPS matters, POCSO cases, or domestic cruelty under Section 498A. Every situation requires timely and strategic legal action.

2. When Do You Need a Criminal Lawyer?

You should contact a criminal lawyer immediately if:

  • You received a police notice or summon
  • An FIR has been filed against you
  • You want to file a complaint or FIR
  • You need anticipatory or regular bail
  • You are falsely accused or targeted unfairly
  • You are a victim of any crime

3. What Our Criminal Lawyers Do

From the moment you reach out, we take charge of your defence. We evaluate your case, explain the law clearly, and prepare a solid legal strategy. Whether it's FIR defence, complaint drafting, securing bail, or courtroom representation, we ensure your rights are never compromised.
